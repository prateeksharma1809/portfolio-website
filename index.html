More about me

I'm deeply passionate about using technology to solve problems and make a positive impact on the world. My educational journey in Electronics and Communications, followed by a Master’s in Computer Science at Rochester Institute of Technology, has been driven by my curiosity and a continuous desire to learn and innovate.

In my professional roles at Amdocs and Infosys, I focused on creating solutions that streamline processes and enhance data quality. I enjoy the challenge of improving operational efficiency, whether it's through developing microservices that cut down processing time or automating data verification to enhance accuracy. My work has significantly improved workflows and contributed to better decision-making processes.

My interest in technology extends beyond my professional life. I’ve embarked on personal projects, such as creating a cost-effective 3D surround scanner and developing a safety device, which were not just technical challenges but also opportunities to contribute to societal good. These projects were recognized and awarded, reflecting my commitment to meaningful innovation.

Volunteering at workshops for underprivileged kids and founding a robotics club have been some of the most rewarding experiences of my life. These activities allowed me to use my skills to inspire and educate, promoting technology as a force for good and empowerment.

At the core, I see myself as a lifelong learner and an innovator, always looking for new ways to apply technology for positive change. My journey is about more than just professional achievements; it's about making a difference, one solution at a time.
